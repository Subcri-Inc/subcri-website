About Subcri

Subcri Industrial Fabricator Company is a specialized manufacturer with a strong reputation in the production of high-quality moulded rubber products, metal fabrication, and engineering plastics. With years of experience in the industry, Subcri Industrial Fabricator has established itself as a trusted partner for various sectors, including automotive parts, construction, and industrial equipment. The company's expertise lies in delivering custom solutions that meet the stringent requirements of its clients, utilizing advanced technology and skilled craftsmanship. By focusing on precision, durability, and innovation, Subcri continues to lead in providing reliable industrial components that support the evolving needs of its clients.

Our Services

Moulded Rubber Products

At Subcri Industrial Fabrication Company, our moulded rubber products are engineered for resilience and performance. We offer a comprehensive range, including manhole gaskets, rubber sleeves, dock fenders, column guard rubber, rollers, rubber bearing pads, gaskets, bellows, oil seals, O-rings, sleeves, belts, and silicone rubber. Each product is crafted to withstand demanding conditions, ensuring long-lasting durability and reliability for your industrial needs.

Metal Fabrication

At Subcri Industrial Fabrication Company, our metal fabrication services are designed to deliver precision and durability. We produce a wide range of high-quality metal products, including gears, pipings, flanges, fabricated parts, springs, steel pallets, steel racks, stainless tanks, and flat forms. Each product is crafted to meet exacting standards, ensuring reliability and performance for your industrial applications.

Engineering Plastics

our At Subcri Industrial Fabrication Company, engineering plastics are designed for high performance and precision.
