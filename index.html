About Subcri

Subcri is an industrial fabricator specialising in moulded rubber products, precision metal fabrication and engineering-grade plastics. We partner with manufacturers and OEMs to turn concepts into durable parts and long-lasting solutions.

Our Services

Moulded Rubber Products

Injection & compression moulding, custom compounds, vibration mounts, seals and precision rubber parts engineered for performance.

Metal Fabrication

Cutting, bending, welding, CNC machining and surface treatments for structural and precision metal components.

Engineering Plastics

High-performance thermoplastics and composite fabrication for components that require strength, chemical resistance and low weight.

Capabilities & Equipment

  • Compression & Injection Moulding
  • Laser Cutting & Waterjet
  • Robotic Welding & MIG/TIG
  • Tooling & Prototyping
